My Story

Hi, I’m Hayley! A junior software developer from Ottawa, Canada, with a passion for UI/UX design and development. After graduating from uOttawa with a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering, I worked for a military tech startup as an Embedded Software Architect. As the first employee hired, I had the opportunity to work on their product in the very early stages of development, and I really enjoyed contributing to design decisions including prototyping and implementing the entire UI for the Android application.

I also worked a part-time serving job to get myself out of the house after my 9-5. This eventually became my full-time job so that I could pursue my dreams of traveling more post-university. In the last year alone, I’ve visited Greece, Italy, France, England, Portugal, the Dominican Republic, British Columbia, New York, Wisconsin, and explored my hometown province of Ontario. I’ve always loved immersing myself in different cultures, and I plan to continue doing so whenever I have the chance.

I’ve always loved art and design. While painting has always been my favourite creative outlet, university introduced me to web and mobile app design that combined my interests and skills in tech design. Check out my favourite paintings here, or discover my portfolio, as well as my resume, or download them both.

When I’m not designing, you might find me golfing, or skiing up at my family cottage in Mont Tremblant.
