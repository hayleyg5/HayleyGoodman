Author Website

I designed and developed this website for my friend Robyn who is an incredible, unpublished author looking to display her work. The website consists of a homepage with her current work, a books page with all of her work, and an about page with a short introduction. It also provides links to her social media (Twitter and Instagram), and a link to send her an email.


Hairdresser Website

I created this website as part of a lab for my university course: Analysis and Design of User Interfaces. This website consists of five sections: Book a Visit, Gallery, Services, Experts, and Contact Us. It uses anchor links in the navbar to jump to each heading on the main page. The footer includes a button to complete a short pop-up survey. Seeing as this was one of my first labs using Bootstrap and jQuery, I had a lot of fun with the design and development process.

Design Patterns: Accordion - Book a Visit, Grid - Services, Card - Experts
